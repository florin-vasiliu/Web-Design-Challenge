Max Temperature

Overall view:

Data collected from cities located all over the world show that the temperature is strongly influenced is influenced by the position on the latitude having an maximum inflection point close to the equator.

Division by hemisphere:

By further dividing the data between the north and the south hemisphere, and performing a linear regression, we obtain a hery high Pearson coeficient which confirms the hypothesis that the temperature is influenced by the latitude.
